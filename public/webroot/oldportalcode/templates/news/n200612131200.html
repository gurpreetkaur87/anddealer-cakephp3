Product Discontinuation

13th December 2006

A&D Mercury wish to advise the discontinuation of two of their product lines.

The two products listed below, are no longer available. Any information that is currently existing on the website will be ammended to reflect this change, but will remain available for informational purposes.

  • SA-15K - Price Computing Scale
  • MC-2000 - Coin & Note Counting Scale

We would like to apologise for any Inconvenience this change to our product line up may cause, however this change was unaviodable.
