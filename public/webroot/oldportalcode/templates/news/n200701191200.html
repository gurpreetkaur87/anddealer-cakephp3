A&D Mercury Celebrated 60th Birthday Year

19th January 2007

In 2006 A&D Mercury celebrated 60 years of serving the Australian Weighing industry. To mark this event we have put together a short history about the company we hope you'll find interesting. We've even included an image of some of the original buildings that occupied the Adelaide factory site in 1912. See the "About" page for details.

It's now time to look at the future and see what the next 60 years will bring, and more importantly, what we can bring to you.
