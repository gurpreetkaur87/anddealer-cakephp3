A&D Mercury Welcomes New Technician

26th October 2007

A&D Mercury welcomed a new qualified technician to the Service Department when Matthew Warne completed his apprenticeship recently.

Matthew, who most of our dealers would recognise from his organised and helpful manner over the phone, has been with the company for 4 years commencing his employment in 2003.

A very popular member of the A&D Mercury team, Matthew has only recently returned from a 6 week holiday to Europe where he took in a great deal of the local culture and quite a bit of the local beverage.

Tom Armstrong, the MD at A&D Mercury, presented Matthew with an award at a ceremony involving all staff at the Adelaide head office, where Mathew is based.

Matthew is the first apprentice that A&D Mercury have had complete their training period in many years and such has been Matthew’s success that the company has now committed to more apprentices in coming years.

A keen sportsman, Matthew has had several periods of absence during his apprenticeship due to a catalogue of injuries, a broken arm received playing football being the most notable, he also competes in the turf cricket competition during the summer.

If you have the opportunity to speak with Matthew in the service department take the chance to congratulate him on the achievement.

For those of you who only know Matthew by voice you can now put a face to the name by the photo attached.

Congratulations Matthew from all at A&D Mercury!
