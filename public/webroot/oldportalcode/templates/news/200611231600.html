New FX-i Precision Balance

23rd November 2006

A&D Mercury is proud to announce the release of the exciting new FX-i Precision Top Pan Balance range.

The FX-i joins the extremely successful GX and GF series to form the most extensive top pan balance range in Australia.

While jam packed with features, the FX-i is around 40% lighter and up to 47% smaller than most of it’s competitiors.

Traditional A&D functions such as Piece counting , Visual Comparator, Percentage Mode and Animal Weighing are all present in this impressive package. On top of the traditional A&D functionality there are new innovative features such as a Statistical Calculation Function, optional USB interface and an optional Ethernet interface.

An environmental setting ensures that the FX-i will always provide optimum performance regardless of the location in which it is used, while a standard RS232C Data interface means your FX-i can output data to a peripheral device such as a printer or P.C., directly out of the box.

A cobalt blue Vacuum Fluorescent Display ensures that the FX-i can be read from any viewing angle, while an Auto Power Off feature will ensure maximum life of the optional rechargeable battery.

A simple calibration routine, using external masses, makes for easy maintenance.

A range of 15 different measurement units ensures the FX-i will provide the answer to your particular weighing requirement, while the underhook facility makes the balance ideal for density measurement or for weighing materials with magnetic properties.

With the features outlined, it’s clear that the FX-i provides unparalleled value for money.
