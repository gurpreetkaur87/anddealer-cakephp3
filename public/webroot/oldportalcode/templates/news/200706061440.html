FS-i Approval

6th of June 2007

A&D Mercury is proud to announce the release of the exciting new FS-i Wet Area Checkweighing Scale range.

A number of fantastic features are available on the FS-i including ultra fast weighing, a 100 position memory function and a rechargeable battery as standard.

High visibilty indicator lights and the Analog Sweep display, make the FS-i the obvious choice for any product packaging or grading requirement.

3 weighing ranges within each capacity make the FS-i an extremely flexible weighing unit which can be configured for your particular application.

The FS-i comes in three separate capacities of 6 kg, 15 kg or 30 kg.

Peripheral equipment address presents no problem for the FS-i with RS232/Relay and RS422/485/Relay options available.

The UFC data formatting function allows the data to be configured to your particular requirement, allowing for accurate placement onto adhesive labels etc.

Take the time to inspect the FS-i, we’re sure you won’t be disappointed.
