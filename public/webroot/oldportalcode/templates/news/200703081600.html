Website Updates

8th of March 2007

We have just updated the website with a new category in the scales section and a new form for requesting a service or repair on your weighing equipment.

A New "Value Weighing Products" category has been added to the Scales section of the website. To launch this category we're featuring the all new SJ-HS Bench Scale as the categories top product. We have also been listening to your feedback and have moved the SK & SK-WP Series "Balances" into this new "Value Weighing Products" to make them easier to find.

Our service manager has been working hard to make it easier to arrange a service or repair on your weighing equipment. To help him acheive that goal we have added a "Service & Repair" item to the left hand menu. Simply fill in your details and a description of the work required and our Service Staff will take care of the rest.
