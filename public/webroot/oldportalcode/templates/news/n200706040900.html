A&D Mercury are pleased to announce the launch of their new website.

4th June 2007

The new format features the functionality that has made the ADM site so popular with our customers, combined with an attractive and professional presentation.

A number of new areas are available on the site including a Service and Repair Request Page and a Frequently Asked Questions section. New products are also featured including the new FX-i Precision Top Pan balance.

A further highlight of the new site is the addition of an In Line Checkweighers section which showcases the various models available in the Dolphin Checkweigher series. Full colour brochures are available for virtually all models on display including new entries such as the BCS Bariatric Chair Scale and PW-200 Personal Scale.

A simple navigation and description system ensures you have fast and easy access to the information that you need. Trade approval information is readily available for all A&D Mercury models to assist with certification issues.

We hope you are able to take the time to look around the new site and familiarise yourself with it’s features and, as always, we welcome any feedback from you with regards to improvements to the site.
