Engineering Note # HVW-G_05

Equipment - HVW-G series scales

Problem - Error 1 display - error codes are not given in the Instruction manual.

Detail

Error 1 was displayed after calibration of the scale and immediately on power on. The scale would not weigh. If the calibration disable routine, F0, was entered and exited the scale would then work until next switched off/on.

Explanation

Error 1 indicates a fault in the Non Volatile Memory and in this instance was caused by a grossly incorrect figure inadvertently being entered for the gravity setting when performing calibration. The figure entered was outside the limits placed in the software as a check on the validity of stored data and caused the error 1 to be displayed.

Avoidance

To avoid this error simply ensure that valid figures are entered for the gravity setting when performing calibration.
