Engineering Note # 4323_01

Equipment - AD4323 OP-07 Analogue option

Problem - Set up procedure not in the Maintenance manual

Procedure

  1. Connect a 500ohm 1/2 watt, low temperature coefficient, resistor across the analogue option output terminals.
  2. Enter Check Mode as below
  1. With the AC power OFF, set the MODE switch on the rear panel to MODE 2.
  2. Set all 3 of the front dip switches to ON.
  3. Press and hold key switches 5 & 6 and switch ON the AC power.

The display will show the software version number, type and country setting followed by 'Option'.
  • Press switch 4 - Display shows OP-07
  • Press switch 1 - Display shows OP-07 4
    At this step 4mA will be output from the option card BNC socket. Measure the voltage with a high impedance digital multimeter. The voltage at 4mA will be 2.000V
  • Press switch 2 - Display shows OP-07 20
    At this step 20mA will be output from the option card BNC socket. Measure the voltage as above, this should be 10.000V
  • If the above voltages are not accurate, adjust R10 on the OP07 board (towards the rear of the indicator) to achieve a difference of 8.000V between step 5 and step 6 measurements.
  • Adjust R11 so that the voltages are 2.000V at 4mA and 10.000V at 20mA, using switch 1 and switch 2 to toggle between the two current settings.

Linearity Test

After completing the adjustments check the linearity using the procedure below.

  1. Press Switch 3 or Switch 4 until the display shows OP-07 2.
  2. Check the linearity in accordance with the table below, using Switch 3 to increase and Switch 4 to reduce the current.
