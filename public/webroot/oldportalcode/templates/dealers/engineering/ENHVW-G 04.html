Engineering Note # HVW-G_04

Equipment - HVW-G series scales

Problem - An undocumented feature of the software settings allows the Cal routines to be disabled.

Detail

A hidden F function - F0 has 2 settings. F0-0 Calibration access is enabled; F0-1 Calibration access is disabled.

To change F0 setting:-

    1. Ensure that the scale is OFF.
    2. Hold the CAL, F and ZERO switches and then press the ON/OFF switch.
      [Scale will display F1-0 or F1-1 depending upon previous selection].
    3. Press the ZERO switch.
      [Scale will display F0-0 or F0-1 depending upon previous selection].
    4. Use the RANGE (HV-G) or SAMPLE (HW-G) switch to select the desired setting.
    5. Press the F switch to complete the sequence and then press the ENTER switch to return to normal weighing.
