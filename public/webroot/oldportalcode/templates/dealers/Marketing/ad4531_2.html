Marketing Tip

Subject AD-4531 Digital Indicator - Option OP-07 fitting

  Please note that the analogue option OP-07 is regarded by ADC as a factory fitted only option. I am sure this is not a practical method for most of our dealers so I have obtained the fitting and set up instructions for this option.
  Installation of OP-07 to the AD-4531
1. Connect the OP-07 to J3 on the lower p.c.b. Looking from the front of the unit J3 is located on the left.
2. Screw through the p.c.b. into the support post of the option
3. Set switch S1 to ON. Looking from the front of the unit S1 should be switched to the right.
4. Cut the connection JP1, which is situated to the right of SW1.
5. Remove the 2 rear panels and connect them direct to the p.c.b.s.
6. Connect a precision 100 ohm resistor across pins 15 and 16 and inject an 8 V DC supply as shown in the circuit below.

 

..............8V DC

7. Activate the Check Mode F16 07.
8. Press ENTER while the ROM version is displayed.
9. The display will toggle between 0 and 2 when the UP arrow is pressed.
10. When 0 is displayed use VR2 to set 0.4V ± 0.4mV across the resistor.
11. When 2 is displayed use VR1 to set 2.0V ± 0.4mV across the resistor.
12. Re-assemble the unit and set up OP-07 as detailed in the Instruction Manual.
