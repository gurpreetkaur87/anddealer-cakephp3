Marketing Tip

Subject AD-4531 Digital Indicator

The method of setting the mains transformer tappings to 120 volt working is not shown
clearly in the technical information.

The table below should be used to set the AD-4531 mains input tapping.

240 Volt Terminal Wire Colour
  TT1 -
  TT2 WHITE
` TT3 VIOLET
  TT4 GREY
  TT5 -
  TT6 ORANGE
  TT7 BROWN
110/120 Volt Terminal Wire Colour
  TT1 VIOLET
  TT2 -
  TT3 -
  TT4 GREY
  TT5 WHITE
  TT6 ORANGE
  TT7 BROWN
100 Volt Terminal Wire Colour
  TT1 VIOLET
  TT2 -
  TT3 -
  TT4 ORANGE
  TT5 BROWN
  TT6 GREY
  TT7 WHITE
