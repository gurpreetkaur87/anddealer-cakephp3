Marketing Tip

Subject - EK-G Balances

Please note that early versions of this balance have a problem with data communication when using a serial data option. The effect is that the balance will turn itself OFF during data communication after a random period of around 30~40 seconds. The balance works quite normally in a stand alone situation. The offending software version is identified as P-2.00 or earlier and the fault was corrected in version P-2.10 or later. To check the software version of an EK-G balance simply hold the CAL switch depressed while turning on the balance.
