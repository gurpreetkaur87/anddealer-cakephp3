Marketing Tip

Subject - SF/SG Calibration Lock Out

  Please note that ADC have updated the software for these retail scales and you may find that the Calibration routines are not accessable by pressing the CAL switch.

ADC have included, at the request of the Asian market, a software lock out for the calibration routine. To overcome this lock out the following procedure must be performed before access to the calibration routine is allowed:

1. Turn off the scale at the power supply. The power must be off not just the display turned off by the front panel push button.
2. Press and hold the 9 button, on the 10 key numeric key pad on the right hand side of the keyboard, and turn on the power.
3. The display will show “000” after a few seconds, on the unit price display.
4. Enter the number 685 and press the MEM button. The scale then shows “------” for a few seconds and then returns to normal weighing mode.
5. The calibration function is now enabled and the instructions in the Calibration Manual can now be followed.
  To reinstall the calibration lock out repeat steps 1,2 and at step 3 press MEM when the display shows “000” and then turn off the power.
