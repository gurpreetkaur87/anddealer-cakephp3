Marketing Tip

Subject - EK-G Portable Balance - Software version P2.11

  Please note that the software has been updated in this balance to version P2.11 but the Instruction Manual has not yet been revised.
  The change is the addition of function settings to enable/disable the Zero Tracking of the instrument.
Function F1 now has these settings:

F1=0

 The Auto Power Off is not used & Zero Tracking is ON

F1=1

 The Auto Power Off is used & Zero Tracking is ON

F1=2

 The Auto Power Off is not used & Zero Tracking is OFF

F1=3

 The Auto Power Off is used & Zero Tracking is OFF
  If the Zero Tracking is enabled it will track Gross and Net zeros.
  To check the software version fitted to any balance start with the balance Off, press and hold the Cal button and then turn the balance On. The software version will be displayed.
  The ability to disable the Zero Tracking is useful when weighing in small increments such as adding dyes to a liquid, slowly adding powders to a formula etc. Without this feature a balance may simply zero track the additions and display incorrect results.
