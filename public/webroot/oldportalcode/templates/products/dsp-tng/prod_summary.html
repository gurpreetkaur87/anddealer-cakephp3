A&D D.S.P/T.N.G Measurement and Control Systems

The New Generation of DSP equipment was designed specifically for the Programmable Measurement and Control System markets.

A&D Mercury has a range of models to choose from, with a full compliment of option cards available to suit the requirements and needs of many different industries. The AD4820 utlises a SH4 7750R 32 Bit RISC processor, running a real time OS, RT Linux. The 4820 is programmed using Machine Ladder & Virtual Console via a 10 Base-T Ethernet port, the AD4820 can handle 256 I/O.
