PRIVACY STATEMENT

A&D Mercury recognises the importance of privacy and of safeguarding the personal information of users of our website. Any personal information provided to A&D Mercury will be protected in accordance with the National Privacy Principles.

We will not collect or monitor personal information without your consent nor rent or sell your name, address, email address or personal information without your permission in advance. However, if the situation arises where there is a legal requirement to disclose information about a customer, then it is our policy to co-operate fully.

So that we can effectively assist your use of the site, we will use your personal information to provide our services and reply to your queries or requests for service. We may occasionally update you with new releases and special opportunities. Please let us know if you do not wish to receive this information and your name will be removed from our mailing list.

We want to develop a continuing and trusting relationship with you so please do not hesitate to contact our office if you need further information regarding our Privacy Policy.
