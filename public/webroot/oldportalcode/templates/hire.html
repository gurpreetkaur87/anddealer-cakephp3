A&D Scale & Equipment Hire & Hire to Own

A&D Mercury offers a large range of weighing equipment available for short or long term hire, as well as our new Hire to Own option.

Although hiring allows you to acquire equipment you require today without a large upfront cash outlay, many customers like to have the option of owning their equipment.

To assist our customers A&D now offers the option of owning your equipment after a mutually agreed minimum hire period (typically 24 months). The equipment may then be purchased by paying an amount equal to one month's hire. For a total of 25 payments it's yours. There's no obligation to purchase however the minimum hire periods apply.

A&D Mercury hire equipment includes Counting Scales, Pallet Scales, Platform Scales, Compact Balances, Digital Indicators, Moisture Analyser's and Postal Scales.

All hire equipment is checked and calibrated prior to dispatch. A calibration certificate can be provided for quality assurance purposes if required.

For further information on any of our hire options, please fill in our contact form.

The Benefits of Hiring
No Large Cash Outlay Hiring allows you to acquire equipment you require today without a large upfront cash outlay. You only need to pay your first hire installment.
No expensive repairs. All hire equipment that fails due to product failure will be repaired free of charge, for the length of the hire period.
Ability to upgrade. Upgrade to the latest equipment to keep up with changes in technology or as your requirements change. (Once you have completed the agreed hire period).
Tax deductible payments. If you are hiring solely for business purposes your hire payments may be 100% tax deductible.
You can apply to buy at any time. You can apply to buy the products you are hiring at any time.
Keep track of your accounts. You can hire multiple products all on the one A&D Mercury account.
Convenient payment options. C.O.D. (cash or cheque) Credit Cards. (Visa, Master Card & Amex) Direct Bank Deposit.
